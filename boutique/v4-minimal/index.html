The Art Collaboration

Treehouse × SoHo Galleries.

A curated art program rotates between our two houses in Mérida. Treehouse is the program; SoHo Galleries is the partner. Tree House Hotel — our sister property in Santa Ana — is the namesake venue, where guests live inside the exhibition.

Here at Boutique by The Museo, you'll see selected works in the public spaces, chosen to sit quietly alongside the restored hacienda.
