The catalog premise is now load-bearing — the rooms get the same caption stack as the artworks; the installation index does work the plates don't; the reviews finally sound like five different people; the Spanish names what is Mexican and only what is Mexican; the CTAs are catalog verbs (`ENTER`, `TAKE`, `ENROLL`) instead of hospitality verbs (`RESERVE`, `SEND`); the manifesto makes specific claims instead of slogan-deck negations.

The two strongest moments R1 surfaced (manifesto coda + the luggage rack sentence) are preserved untouched. The rest of the page now reaches up toward them rather than falling away.
