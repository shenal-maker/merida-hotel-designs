04 / LA EXPOSICIÓN — VOL. III, EL JARDÍN INTERIOR
CURRENT EXHIBITION VOL. III

EL JARDÍN interior

OPENING 15 MAY 2026 CLOSING 14 SEP 2026
CURATED BY TREEHOUSE × SOHO GALLERIES (MÉRIDA) — ON VIEW THROUGHOUT THE HOUSE
— CURATORIAL STATEMENT

The hotel is the gallery. Fifteen rooms, one courtyard, and a rotating slate of work installed where one ordinarily expects only a luggage rack and a thermostat. Each of the six works in El Jardín interior was made for, or relocated into, a domestic space the artist did not own: a borrowed kitchen, a hotel room, a friend's stairwell. The exhibition argues that a domestic interior is not the opposite of a public one — it is a public one we have agreed to call private.

Works are installed throughout the property — above the breakfast table, beside the bathtub, at the foot of bed № 7 — and rotate quarterly. Guests live inside the show. The catalog ships at checkout.

— S. RAMÍREZ & J. CASARES, CO-CURATORS MÉRIDA, ABR. 2026
