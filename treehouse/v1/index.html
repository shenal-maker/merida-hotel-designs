An Ongoing Collaboration · Namesake Venue

TreehousexSoHo Galleries

Treehouse is the name of a curated art program with SoHo Galleries, Mérida. This house is its namesake venue — work lives in the corridors, on the courtyard ledges, at the foot of the stairs. The rooms are not its backdrop. They are the room.
