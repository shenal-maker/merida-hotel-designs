The Art Collaboration

Treehouse × SoHo Galleries.

Tree House Hotel is the namesake venue for Treehouse — a curated art program in partnership with SoHo Galleries, Mérida. Rotating works live in the bar, the lounge, the corridors. Guests live inside the exhibition.

It is the only thing about the hotel that changes. Everything else is the property, the canopy, the quiet.
